AdZU SHS Research Archive Website Guidelines

Purpose of the Website

This website serves as the official digital archive for research outputs produced by Senior High School students of Ateneo de Zamboanga University. It aims to:

  • Provide a centralized platform for storing and showcasing student research.
  • Encourage knowledge sharing and academic collaboration within the AdZU community.
  • Preserve and promote scholarly output from AdZU SHS.

Access and Authentication

AdZU Account Requirement

  • Use the @adzu.edu.ph email address to access or download research files.

Account Verification

  • Guests and external users will be able to see the research files abstract but will not be permitted to access full research documents.

Uploading and Archiving

Submission Protocol

  • Research outputs must be submitted in PDF format, accompanied by metadata: title, author(s), strand, school year, and abstract.
  • All files must be approved by an SHS Research Coordinator or Faculty Adviser before they are published.

File Organization

  • Research papers will be categorized by:
    • Strand (e.g., STEM, ABM, HUMSS, GAS)
    • Academic Year
    • Type of Output (e.g., Capstone Project, Action Research)

File Naming Convention

  • Use the format: YEAR_STRAND_LASTNAME_TITLE.pdf
    • Example: 2025_STEM_DelaCruz_ClimateChangeMitigation.pdf

Browsing and Searching

Search Filters

  • Users can filter by:
    • Strand
    • Academic Year
    • Keywords or Tags
    • Research Title or Author

Abstract View

  • Users can preview the research abstract before accessing the full document.

Downloading and Usage Policy

Download Restrictions

  • Only AdZU users can download files. An AdZU email login is required for every download session.

Academic Integrity

  • Users must NOT plagiarize, alter, or misuse the research content.
  • Proper citation is mandatory when using the work for academic or reference purposes.

Citation Format (Recommended)

Lastname, Firstname. (Year). Title of the Research. Ateneo de Zamboanga University SHS Research Archive.

Maintenance and Updates

Content Review

  • The archive will be periodically reviewed for outdated or incorrect files.
  • Authors can request updates or takedowns by emailing the SHS Research Office.

System Maintenance

  • Scheduled maintenance will be announced via the AdZU portal or email.
  • The system will have regular backups to ensure data security.

Prohibited Use

  • Sharing research files outside the AdZU network without permission.
  • Using archived research for commercial gain or without attribution.
  • Tampering with, modifying, or misrepresenting student work.

Contact and Support

For questions or technical support, email: shsresearch@adzu.edu.ph

For access issues, contact: itsupport@adzu.edu.ph
